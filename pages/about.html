About Us

We are a community of aspiring and existing Product Managers. We are San Francisco-based, with a mission to educate and connect product managers. We focus on learning skills from the best practitioners in the field. Stay connected via our blog.

Are you a leader? If you would like to propose an event or collaborate, please share your idea via this survey. Thank you for your interest and time!
